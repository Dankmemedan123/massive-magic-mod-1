# ARCANA — World Design: Biomes & Structures
# Goal: A player should NEVER run out of new things to discover.
# Every biome is visually distinct. Every structure has unique gameplay.

---

## BIOME COUNT: 24 total (8 overworld + 4×4 dimension)

---

## OVERWORLD BIOMES (8) — Each tied to a magic school

### 1. Enchanted Forest (Luxomancy)
- **Terrain:** Rolling hills, giant Enchanted Oak trees (3x normal size), 
  crystal flower patches, small ponds with glowing lily pads
- **Unique blocks:** Enchanted Planks, Enchanted Leaves (emit light level 7), 
  Mana Grass (sparkles), Crystal Flowers (harvestable for mana dust)
- **Vegetation:** Oversized ferns, luminous mushrooms, Mana Berry Bushes
- **Ambient:** Golden mana particles drifting upward, warm ambient glow even at night,
  firefly-like particles near trees, gentle wind sound
- **Mobs:** Mana Butterflies (passive, drop mana dust), Crystal Deer (neutral, 
  drop crystal shards when fed wheat), Enchanted Fox (finds buried gems)
- **Structures:** Mana Springs, Enchanted Groves, hidden fairy rings (small loot circles)
- **Unique resource:** Mana Berries (only bush spawn location), Light Essence (higher drop rate)
- **FEEL:** Studio Ghibli forest — magical, warm, safe-feeling but full of secrets

### 2. Crystal Wastes (Geomancy)
- **Terrain:** Flat desert-like terrain but made of Crystal Sand (lightly glowing),
  massive crystal formations jutting from the ground (5-15 blocks tall),
  shallow crystal pools, geode caves beneath surface
- **Unique blocks:** Crystal Sand, Crystal Stone, Exposed Crystal Cluster, 
  Crystal Glass (natural transparent blocks)
- **Vegetation:** None above ground — crystalline formations replace trees.
  Underground: luminescent crystal moss
- **Ambient:** Rainbow refractions on nearby blocks when sun hits crystals,
  tinkling crystal sounds, occasional harmonic hum
- **Mobs:** Crystal Golem (hostile, drops crystal shards), Mana Wisp (small, 
  projectile attack), Crystal Deer (rare, neutral)
- **Structures:** Crystal Cave Entrance (leads to large underground geode chamber),
  Ancient Crystal Forge (pre-built forge with rare gem loot)
- **Unique resource:** All 5 Mana Crystal tiers naturally occur, Crystalite Ore exposed
- **FEEL:** Alien landscape — beautiful but inhospitable. Glass desert at sunset.

### 3. Shadow Marsh (Umbramancy)
- **Terrain:** Low, swampy, perpetual fog (render distance reduced to 6 chunks),
  dark water pools, dead trees with hanging moss, mud patches
- **Unique blocks:** Shadow Moss, Void Stone outcrops, Dark Water (damages on contact),
  Corrupted Wood (dark purple wood)
- **Vegetation:** Dead trees, hanging vines, glowing fungi (dim purple light),
  thorn bushes (damage on contact)
- **Ambient:** Thick purple-black fog, whisper sounds, occasional shadow tendrils 
  visible in peripheral vision, sky is always dark even during day
- **Mobs:** Shadow Wraith (teleports, fast, night-only in other biomes but ALWAYS here),
  Void Crawler (ambush from mud), Shadow Hound (pack hunter, 3-5 per pack)
- **Structures:** Dark Shrine (shadow essence loot + Voidstone), 
  Sunken Crypt (underground, skeleton mob spawner + shadow lore books),
  Witch's Hut (NPC alchemist lives here, sells shadow spells)
- **Unique resource:** Shadow Essence (3x normal drop rate), Voidstone Ore (surface level)
- **FEEL:** Darkroot Garden (Dark Souls) — oppressive, dangerous, rewarding

### 4. Starfall Peaks (Electromancy)
- **Terrain:** Extreme mountain biome with jagged peaks reaching y=200+,
  floating rock formations tethered by energy streams, 
  thin atmosphere (sky is darker, stars visible during day)
- **Unique blocks:** Starfall Stone (emits light 3, gravity-defying), 
  Charged Stone (redstone-like pulse), Storm Crystal clusters
- **Vegetation:** Alpine crystal trees (short, hardy, glow during storms),
  star moss on rock faces
- **Ambient:** Frequent thunderstorms (3x normal rate), aurora borealis effect at night,
  lightning strikes are more common, static electricity particles near peaks
- **Mobs:** Elemental Sprite (lightning variant, common), Storm Eagle (neutral flying mob,
  drops Star Feather), Charged Skeleton (Arcane Skeleton + lightning)
- **Structures:** Sky Altar (floating platform at y=200+, Wind Opal + Lightning Topaz loot),
  Storm Spire (tall tower, Electromancy scrolls), Crashed Meteor (small crater with Starmetal)
- **Unique resource:** Starmetal Ore (mountain-only), Lightning Topaz, Storm Alloy components
- **FEEL:** Archdragon Peak (Dark Souls 3) — epic scale, elemental danger, breathtaking views

### 5. Scorched Barrens (Pyromancy)  
- **Terrain:** Cracked basalt ground, lava rivers, smoldering terrain with smoke columns,
  charred tree stumps, obsidian formations, fire geysers (random fire bursts)
- **Unique blocks:** Scorched Earth (damages if stood on too long), Ember Stone 
  (emits fire particles), Obsidian Spikes, Ash Block
- **Vegetation:** Fire Ferns (immune to fire, harvestable for Fire Essence),
  Ember Bloom flowers (light source), Charcoal Mushrooms
- **Ambient:** Heat shimmer effect (distortion shader), smoke particles rising from ground,
  random small fire patches, orange-tinted sky
- **Mobs:** Elemental Sprite (fire variant), Magma Wisp, Fire Beetle (new mob — 
  armored, charges, immune to fire, drops Ember Stone)
- **Structures:** Dragon Roost (overworld dragon egg + Draconic loot), 
  Volcanic Forge (pre-built Arcane Forge + fire alloy recipes),
  Flame Shrine (Pyromancy scrolls + Fire Ruby)
- **Unique resource:** Fire Ruby, Sunsteel Ore (exposed), Fire Essence (ambient drops)
- **FEEL:** Mordor meets Mustafar — dangerous but full of fire-aligned treasures

### 6. Frozen Sanctum Tundra (Cryomancy)
- **Terrain:** Deep snow, frozen rivers and waterfalls (ice blocks), 
  ice spire formations, permafrost caves, aurora sky at night
- **Unique blocks:** Enchanted Ice (doesn't melt near heat), Frost Crystal Cluster,
  Packed Snow (harder than regular), Glacial Stone
- **Vegetation:** Crystal Pines (ice-covered trees, drop Frost Sapphire leaves),
  Frost Flowers (harvestable for Ice Essence), Ice Moss
- **Ambient:** Snowfall (even when weather is clear elsewhere), 
  crystalline wind chime sounds, breath particles visible, 
  slight blue tint to all lighting
- **Mobs:** Frost Strider (new — ice spider that creates ice trail), 
  Snow Elemental (slow, tanky, throws ice boulders),
  Arctic Fox (tameable, tracks nearby ores)
- **Structures:** Frozen Sanctum (ice temple with Frost Sapphire + Cryomancy scrolls),
  Ice Caverns (underground maze with frozen chests), 
  Yeti Den (mini-boss: Snow Elemental Alpha, drops Frost Heart)
- **Unique resource:** Frost Sapphire, Moonsilver Ore (higher rate), Ice Essence
- **FEEL:** Ice Crown (WoW) — majestic, desolate, hidden beauty under the frost

### 7. Whispering Thicket (Aeromancy)
- **Terrain:** Dense bamboo-like tall grass (8+ blocks high), floating pollen orbs,
  terrain has many ravines with wind updrafts, natural arches and bridges
- **Unique blocks:** Windwood (flexible wood, sways), Pollen Block (slow fall when near),
  Cloud Block (walkable but semi-transparent)
- **Vegetation:** Sky Vines (climb upward toward floating islands), 
  Wind Chime Flowers (make sound when wind blows), Feathered Ferns
- **Ambient:** Strong wind sound effects, leaves blow horizontally, 
  pollen particles drift on wind currents, natural updrafts in ravines 
  (player gets launched upward — can use with Elytra!)
- **Mobs:** Wind Sprite (passive, pushed by wind), Storm Hawk (neutral, drops 
  Wind Opal on rare occasion), Breeze Wisp (tiny, lights the way)
- **Structures:** Windmill Tower (NPC sage, sells Aeromancy scrolls),
  Floating Garden (sky island at y=180, Earth Emerald + rare loot),
  Wind Temple (underground, puzzle-based with pressure plate + wind mechanics)
- **Unique resource:** Wind Opal, Earth Emerald (in floating formations), Wind Essence
- **FEEL:** Breath of the Wild Faron region — vertical, wind-driven, exploration-focused

### 8. Arcane Nexus (Arcanomancy — RARE biome)
- **Terrain:** Small biome (like Mushroom Island rarity). Terrain made of Arcane Stone
  with glowing runic patterns embedded in the ground. Floating geometric shapes 
  orbit above. Reality seems to glitch — blocks shimmer between states.
- **Unique blocks:** Runic Stone Glowing, Paradox Block (appears to be two blocks 
  at once — visual effect), Arcane Conduit (natural mana channel)
- **Vegetation:** No natural plants — instead, geometric crystal "trees" made of 
  pure mana that pulse with energy
- **Ambient:** ALL mana particles from all 8 schools visible simultaneously,
  surreal color palette, distant hum of raw magical energy,
  time feels different (day/night cycle 2x speed in this biome)
- **Mobs:** Arcane Phantom (phases through blocks), Mana Elemental (absorbs mana),
  The Wandering Mage NPC (GUARANTEED spawn in this biome)
- **Structures:** Arcane Library (LARGE — multi-floor, guaranteed high-tier scrolls),
  Portal Ruins (repairable portal to Arcane Realm dimension),
  Nexus Core (center of biome — mana regen 10x in radius, rare gem loot)
- **Unique resource:** Arcane Amethyst (only found here), all essences at low rates
- **FEEL:** The upside-down meets a wizard's fever dream — ultra-rare, ultra-rewarding

## DIMENSION 1: THE ARCANE REALM (4 biomes)

### 9. Mana Gardens
- **Terrain:** Lush floating islands connected by natural bridges, waterfalls 
  that fall into void, rich magical soil, everything glows
- **Unique blocks:** Mana Soil (crops grow 3x speed), Living Stone (slowly regenerates 
  if broken), Radiant Leaf blocks
- **Vegetation:** Mana Trees (massive, canopy contains hidden chests), 
  Glowing Orchids, Mana Lotus (on water — restores mana when walked over)
- **Ambient:** Aurora sky (permanent shifting colors), floating light orbs, 
  gentle music-like ambient tones, mana rain (visible sparkle rain)
- **Mobs:** Luminous Jellyfish (passive, light source), Mana Butterfly swarms,
  Pixie (tiny neutral, trades mana for items if you throw items at them)
- **Structures:** Crystal Palace (large crystal structure, high-tier gem loot),
  Mana Wellspring (infinite mana source block — very rare)
- **FEEL:** Avatar Pandora + Skylands — breathtaking, alien beauty

### 10. Crystal Spires
- **Terrain:** Vertical crystal formations reaching 100+ blocks, narrow paths 
  between spires, crystal bridges, geode caves inside massive crystals
- **Unique blocks:** Prismatic Crystal (all colors cycle through), Resonance Stone 
  (emits sound when stepped on — like note blocks but magical)
- **Ambient:** Crystal harmonics (musical ambient), rainbow light refractions,
  crystal dust particles fall like snow
- **Mobs:** Crystal Beast (charges, shatters into shards on death — those shards 
  become tiny hostile mobs!), Prism Spider, Crystal Bat (passive)
- **Structures:** Crystal Conservatory (puzzle dungeon — align crystals to open doors),
  Geode Heart (center of massive crystal, contains Prismatic Mana Crystal)
- **FEEL:** Crystal Cave from Skyrim × 100 — vertical, dazzling, fragile-feeling

### 11. Aether Highlands
- **Terrain:** Above the clouds — solid cloud platforms with grass on top,
  massive sky whales visible in distance (ambient), golden sunlight
- **Unique blocks:** Cloud Block (solid but can be walked through from below),
  Skystone (lightweight, gravity-defying when powered by mana),
  Aether Grass (light blue-green)
- **Ambient:** Wind at high altitude, cloud formations drift past, occasional 
  rainbow, visibility extends enormously — can see all biomes from here
- **Mobs:** Aether Knight (flying, sword attacks), Star Bird (passive, drops star feathers),
  Cloud Elemental (new — made of cloud, hard to hit, shoots wind bursts)
- **Structures:** Sky Citadel (Archon boss arena — floating fortress),
  Aether Dock (landing platform with NPC merchant),
  Observatory (telescope-like block that reveals nearby structure locations)
- **FEEL:** Kingdom Hearts + Skyrim Sovngarde — ethereal, heavenly, epic scale

### 12. Arcane Wastes
- **Terrain:** Corrupted version of Mana Gardens — mana has gone wrong here.
  Terrain is fractured, floating debris, unstable ground (random block breaks),
  wild mana storms (periodic AoE damage zones that move)
- **Unique blocks:** Corrupted Mana Block (drains player mana on contact),
  Fractured Stone (breaks after being stepped on — like sand over void),
  Wild Mana Crystal (explodes if mined incorrectly — must use silk touch)
- **Ambient:** Chaotic particle effects, discordant sounds, reality glitches 
  (blocks briefly change appearance), lightning without thunder
- **Mobs:** Mana Elemental (hostile here — absorbs your mana), Corrupted Golem,
  Spell Weaver (casts random AoE spells), Arcane Wraith (new — teleports, steals spells temporarily)
- **Structures:** Corrupted Citadel (dungeon — trapped hallways, puzzle rooms, 
  the source of the corruption — destroying it "heals" nearby terrain),
  Mana Rift (portal to secret area with Aethrium Ore concentrated deposit)
- **FEEL:** Corruption from Terraria meets Void from Hollow Knight — hostile, unstable, endgame

## DIMENSION 2: THE SHADOW DEPTHS (4 biomes)

### 13. Fungal Caverns
- **Terrain:** Massive underground cavern with bioluminescent mushroom forests,
  mushroom trees 20+ blocks tall, mycelium floor, spore fog
- **Unique blocks:** Giant Mushroom Cap (walkable), Bioluminescent Mycelium (light 8),
  Spore Block (releases poison particles when broken)
- **Vegetation:** Glowing Shrooms (6 color variants), Sporecap Trees, 
  Luminous Lichen (wall-growing light source)
- **Ambient:** Spore particles floating, dripping water sounds, mushroom 
  ambient glow creates eerie under-canopy lighting, occasional deep rumble
- **Mobs:** Fungal Zombie (slow, poison aura), Mushroom Beetle (neutral until 
  attacked, explodes into spores), Spore Bat (passive, light source)
- **Structures:** Mushroom Village (small NPC hamlet — mushroom folk who trade 
  unique potions), Spore Queen's Lair (mini-boss — drops potion recipes),
  Underground Farm (pre-built, teaches player mushroom farming mechanics)
- **FEEL:** Blackreach from Skyrim — jaw-dropping underground forest

### 14. Void Chasm
- **Terrain:** Narrow bridges and platforms over infinite void, 
  gravity is reduced (higher jumps, slower falls), floating debris,
  void tendrils reach up from below (visual only — aesthetic menace)
- **Unique blocks:** Void Stone (completely black, absorbs light), 
  Void Bridge (transparent purple bridge that only appears when walked on),
  Anchor Block (prevents void pull in 5 block radius)
- **Ambient:** Complete silence broken by distant void rumbles, 
  absence of particles (eerie stillness), edge-of-void wind sound,
  void visual effect at edges (screen darkens looking down)
- **Mobs:** Void Tentacle (emerges from ground, pulls toward void), 
  Void Leech (drains mana AND health), Void Walker (new — humanoid, 
  teleports like Enderman but hostile, drops Void Diamond)
- **Structures:** Void Gate (portal to deeper layer — Eternium Ore here),
  Suspended Library (floating books and shelves — forbidden knowledge lore),
  The Abyss (looking into it triggers a lore event — "something looks back")
- **FEEL:** The End + Subnautica's Void — existential terror, vertigo, isolation

### 15. Shadow Forest
- **Terrain:** Dead black trees with silver leaves, ground is dark grass 
  that absorbs light, sky is a void with a dim purple sun,
  shadows seem to move independently of their sources
- **Unique blocks:** Shadow Wood (absorbs light level — reduces nearby light by 3),
  Silver Leaf blocks, Dark Grass, Soul Lantern (only light source that works fully)
- **Vegetation:** Shadow Ferns, Nightmare Flowers (cause brief hallucination 
  particles when walked past), Silver-leaf Trees
- **Ambient:** Whispering voices (random chat messages from "the shadows"),
  shadows elongate unnaturally, occasional shadow figure visible briefly,
  all light sources are dimmed by 5 levels
- **Mobs:** Shadow Stalker (invisible until 5 blocks away — reveals with distortion effect),
  Shadow Hound (pack, fast), Lich Minion (patrols near Shadow Fortress)
- **Structures:** Shadow Fortress (Lich boss arena — castle with trapped corridors,
  illusion walls, soul-fire traps), Shadow Outpost (small camp, dark knight NPCs),
  Whispering Shrine (interact to gain temporary dark vision + Shadow Essence)
- **FEEL:** Dark Souls 3 Untended Graves — the normal world but WRONG

### 16. Bone Fields
- **Terrain:** Flat terrain made of Bone Blocks, massive skeletal remains of 
  ancient creatures (ribs arching 30+ blocks high), skull formations,
  rivers of green liquid (soul water — damages living, heals undead)
- **Unique blocks:** Bone Block variants (cracked, fossilized, marrow-filled),
  Soul Water (liquid), Ancient Bone (hardened, mineral-rich)
- **Vegetation:** Bone Grass (grey, brittle), Soul Flowers (absorb soul essence 
  from nearby mob deaths — harvestable after absorbing 5 kills)
- **Ambient:** Bone wind chimes from the skeletal arches, ghostly wails in distance,
  green bioluminescence from soul water, cold visual filter
- **Mobs:** Bone Revenant (skeleton with regen — must kill 3x), 
  Bone Colossus (new — massive skeleton that reforms from scattered bones),
  Soul Wisp (passive, attracted to death — collect 10 for a Soul Essence)
- **Structures:** Ossuary (bone structure with Death Essence loot),
  Lich's Crypt (entrance to Shadow Lich pre-boss dungeon — maze + traps),
  Bone Altar (ritual site — sacrifice bones for shadow spell scrolls)
- **FEEL:** Shadow of the Colossus graveyard + Catacombs of Paris — solemn, dangerous, ancient

## DIMENSION 3: THE CELESTIAL SPIRE (4 biomes)

### 17. Star Gardens
- **Terrain:** Floating island archipelago in a nebula sky, 
  crystalline waterfalls that flow upward, star-fruit trees,
  terrain blocks shift through pastel colors slowly
- **Unique blocks:** Star Stone (shifts hue over time), Nebula Glass (semi-transparent, 
  shows stars through it), Celestial Soil (crops grow ANY plant instantly once)
- **Vegetation:** Star Fruit Trees (fruit restores health + mana), 
  Constellation Flowers (form patterns matching real constellations),
  Cosmic Vines (grow sideways, connecting islands)
- **Ambient:** Nebula sky with visible star clusters, gentle cosmic hum,
  shooting stars every few minutes (land nearby — mine for Celestium),
  zero-gravity pockets (random 5x5 areas where you float)
- **Mobs:** Star Bird (passive, drops star feathers), 
  Constellation Beast (neutral — constellation pattern on body, drops Celestium),
  Star Guardian (ranged, holy damage — patrols gardens)
- **Structures:** Celestial Greenhouse (rare plants + seeds unavailable elsewhere),
  Star Well (wish well — throw in items to receive random rare items),
  Constellation Map (interact to see all Celestial Spire structure locations)
- **FEEL:** Outer Wilds + Mario Galaxy — wonder, discovery, cosmic beauty

### 18. Crystal Peaks
- **Terrain:** Mountain spires made entirely of crystal, reaching extreme heights,
  interior is hollow with crystal cave systems, bridges between peaks
- **Unique blocks:** Celestial Crystal (8 color variants), Resonance Bridge 
  (forms when two Celestial Crystals are placed within 10 blocks — 
  a bridge of light connects them)
- **Ambient:** Crystal resonance music, prismatic light effects inside caves,
  crystal dust falls like gentle snow
- **Mobs:** Crystal Dragon (smaller dragon, fire breath, 150 HP mini-boss — 
  multiple spawn in this biome), Light Wisp (heals other mobs — priority target)
- **Structures:** Dragon Aerie (Crystal Dragon nest, eggs that hatch allies if 
  incubated at home with mana), Crystal Forge (advanced crafting station 
  for Tier 5-6 alloys)
- **FEEL:** Crystal Caves of Naica × Crystal Kingdom — breathtaking, treacherous heights

### 19. Solar Plateau
- **Terrain:** Flat, golden terrain bathed in permanent bright sunlight,
  sun is enormous and close (visual only), heat shimmer effect,
  golden fields of energy grass, sun-dial structures
- **Unique blocks:** Sunstone (permanent light 15 + heals nearby players slowly),
  Solar Panel (natural block — powers Celestium ore growth),
  Golden Grass (aesthetic, sparkles)
- **Ambient:** Warm golden glow everywhere, lens flare effect looking at sun,
  peaceful harmonic ambient, butterflies made of light
- **Mobs:** Celestial Golem (massive, slow, 500 HP — drops Celestium Block),
  Solar Phoenix (rare spawn — drops Phoenix Alloy ingredients),
  Eternium Construct (endgame mob, 200 HP, drops Eternium Ingot)
- **Structures:** Celestial Citadel (Dragon boss pre-area — lore + preparation),
  Sun Temple (permanent mana regen zone + Light Essence concentrated),
  Eternium Vault (locked — requires all 3 dimension boss trophies to open,
  contains Eternium Ore deposit + endgame lore)
- **FEEL:** Anor Londo (Dark Souls) — golden, awe-inspiring, deceptively peaceful

### 20. Eternal Dusk
- **Terrain:** Twilight zone between light and dark, terrain is half-golden 
  half-shadow, a visible terminator line separates day and night sides,
  crossing the line shifts the entire visual palette
- **Unique blocks:** Twilight Stone (shifts between light and dark appearance 
  based on which side of the terminator you're on), 
  Eclipse Crystal (combines shadow + light properties)
- **Ambient:** One side has golden particles + warm light, other side has 
  shadow particles + cold blue light. Standing on the terminator line: 
  both effects combine into an otherworldly aurora effect
- **Mobs:** Light side: Star Guardian, Celestial Golem
  Dark side: Shadow Stalker, Void Walker
  Terminator line: Eclipse Sentinel (new — uses both light AND shadow attacks,
  drops Eclipse Crystal, 300 HP)
- **Structures:** Eclipse Temple (where the Eclipse Staff ultimate upgrade is found),
  Dragon's Throne (THE Arcane Dragon boss arena — massive colosseum 
  straddling the terminator line, fight uses both light and dark mechanics),
  Void Incarnate Shrine (hidden, after Dragon — secret boss summon point)
- **FEEL:** Eclipse from Berserk — the boundary between worlds, ultimate confrontation

### Structure Interior Design Principles:
- **EVERY structure has at least 1 puzzle or trap** (not just rooms with chests)
- **EVERY structure has at least 1 lore book** (worldbuilding)
- **EVERY structure has at least 1 unique mob or NPC** (encounter)
- **NO structure is just an empty shell** — all have multiple rooms/levels
- **Structures in the same biome are themed consistently** but vary in layout

## STRUCTURE PUZZLE TYPES (no two structures solve the same way)

| Puzzle Type | Used In | Mechanic |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Crystal Alignment | Crystal Conservatory, Crystal Cave | Rotate crystals to direct light beam to door |
| Pressure Plate Sequence | Wind Temple, Underground Cathedral | Step on plates in correct order (shown by glyphs) |
| Lightning Redirect | Storm Spire | Place conductors to redirect lightning to power source |
| Ice Maze | Frozen Sanctum | Navigate sliding ice + breakable walls |
| Lava Floor | Flame Shrine | Platforming over lava using timed fire geysers |
| Illusion Walls | Shadow Fortress | Walk through fake walls, real walls look fake |
| Rune Matching | Elemental Nexus | Match rune patterns on floor to door glyphs |
| Block Pushing | Arcane Citadel | Push blocks to complete circuit + open gate |
| Music/Sound | Crystal Peaks | Play Resonance Stones in correct order |
| Void Bridge | Void Chasm | Bridges appear only when standing on them — memorize path |
| Light/Dark Toggle | Eclipse Temple | Switch between light and dark to reveal/hide paths |
| Soul Fire | Lich's Crypt | Light soul lanterns in sequence to dispel barriers |
